1.TAJ MAHAL




The is a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is considered one of the world's most famous architectural achievements.
